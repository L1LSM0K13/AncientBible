About Orthodox Canon

This is a Bible website that contains all books of the typical Orthodox canon, as well as other non canonical writings, gnostic texts, the local and ecumenical councils, and the writings of the early church fathers. This website is a work in progress so I will be adding more content to expand the library. This website will also eventually have a fully customizable bible layout with note taking.

Whats included

This website contains all the of the 66 protocanonical books of the Bible, as well as 10 deuterocanonical books from the orthodox canon. For those who are don't know the Orthodox canon, these are the books that are in the canon:
